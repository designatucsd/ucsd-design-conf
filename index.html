FAQ

Who can attend?

All UCSD Undergraduates, Graduates, and Alumni are welcome to attend along with affiliates of the UCSD design community

Do I have to pay for parking?

Parking in A,B,S & V spots on campus are free on the weekends. We suggest parking in Gilman Parking Structure.

What do I need to bring?

Yourself, Resume, Identification (Student ID Preferred) as well as a notepad to take notes!

How much will it cost?

The UC San Diego Design Conference is free of charge for all attendees!

Will food be provided?

Yes! Lunch will be provided and light refreshments will be available throughout the day.

Where and when is the conference?

The conference will be help in Price Center Theater. Registration will begin at 9am and the event will end at 5pm.
